About Us

Learn more about our journey, mission, and commitment to authentic African cuisine.

Our Story

Plate of Africa was founded in 2019 with a vision to bring the authentic flavors of African cuisine to a wider audience, while embracing plant-based cooking methods. Our founder, Chef Amara Okafor, traveled extensively throughout the African continent, learning cooking techniques and recipes passed down through generations.

What began as a small pop-up restaurant quickly gained popularity, and we've since expanded to multiple locations across the Pacific Northwest. Each location maintains the same commitment to quality ingredients and cultural authenticity that has defined us from the beginning.

Our Core Values

Cultural Authenticity

We honor the rich culinary traditions of the African continent, preserving authentic flavors and cooking techniques while innovating for today's diners.

Environmental Stewardship

Our plant-based approach significantly reduces environmental impact. We strive to minimize waste, conserve water, and source ingredients sustainably.

Community Connection

We create spaces where people can connect through food, celebrating the communal aspect of dining that is central to African culture.
