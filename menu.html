Dietary Notes

At Plate of Africa, all our dishes are 100% vegan. We're committed to making our menu accessible to guests with various dietary needs. Please ask your server about:

  • Gluten-free options: Many of our dishes are naturally gluten-free, and others can be adapted.
  • Nut allergies: We can provide detailed allergen information and accommodate nut-free requests for most dishes.
  • Spice levels: The heat level of most dishes can be adjusted according to your preference.

Please inform your server of any allergies or dietary restrictions when ordering. While we take every precaution to accommodate allergies, our kitchen does handle various allergens.
